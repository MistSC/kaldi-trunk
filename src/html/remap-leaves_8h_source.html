23 /*
24  Things in this header relate to transforming an FST whose symbols on the
25  left are sequences of phones (as created in context-fst.h) into a
26  representation where the individual leaves are on arcs
27  (not sequences of leaves). We also introduce a mapping on the leaves, which
28  is intended to allow us to reconstruct the phone sequence (basically, if
29  different phones/HMM-positions share a leaf then we introduce new leaf-ids).
30 
31  The way that this is intended be used is: create C as a special FST as in
32  context-fst.h, create C o L o G (somehow), use functions declared in this
33  header to create essentially H o C o L o G (with remapping but with no
34  self-loops), and then afterwards determinize and factor the result. Then
35  we will create a wrapper class that "expands" the factored form on the
36  fly into a full representation of H o C o L o G with self-loops included
37  and possibly with different leaf-ids to disambiguate self-loops from
38  forward transitions. Note also that this wrapper class may also need
39  to expand leaf-ids that actually represent HMMs rather than single states,
40  as this is a possibility too.
41 */
