Detailed Description

Keywords: natural gradient descent, NG-SGD, naturalgradient.

For the top-level of the natural gradient code look here, and also in nnet-precondition-online.h. NaturalGradientAffineComponent is a version of AffineComponent that has a non-(multiple of unit) learning-rate matrix. See nnet-precondition-online.h for a description of the technique. It is described, under the name Online NG-SGD, in the paper "Parallel training of DNNs with Natural Gradient and Parameter Averaging" (ICLR workshop, 2015) by Daniel Povey, Xiaohui Zhang and Sanjeev Khudanpur.
